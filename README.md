📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes customer shopping behaviour using an interactive Power BI dashboard. It explores revenue patterns, customer demographics, seasonal trends, product preferences, and loyalty program effectiveness — providing actionable insights for business decision-making.

🔍 Key Insights

 1. Gender Spending is Nearly Equal
Female customers account for 45% of total spend (€674K), while male customers contribute 44.66% (€663K). The brand has strong cross-gender appeal — no single group dominates revenue.

 2. No Single Hero Product
The top 5 categories — Refresher, Tea, Frappuccino, Espresso, and Other — are all within €4,000 of each other, ranging from €246K to €249.7K. This shows diverse customer preferences but signals an opportunity to build a dominant signature product.

3. 25–34 Age Group Drives the Most Revenue
The 25–34 age group is the top contributor at €462.95K (≈31%), followed by 35–44 at €353.79K. Together they account for over half of all revenue — making young professionals the core target segment.

4. West Region Leads Geographically

| Region | Revenue |
|---|---|
| West | € 337,395 |
| Southeast | € 300,627 |
| Southwest | € 292,800 |
| Midwest | € 288,626 |
| Northeast | € 267,227 |

5. Suburban Stores Outperform Urban & Rural
Suburban stores generate €0.53M, slightly ahead of Urban (€0.48M) and Rural (€0.47M) — suggesting suburban customers have higher per-visit spending habits.

6. Revenue is Consistent Across All Seasons
Seasonal revenue stays flat at around €168K–€169K per season, showing stable year-round demand. This is a business strength but also highlights untapped seasonal upsell opportunities.

7. Loyalty Program Shows No Clear Spending Lift
Rewards members (€0.75M) and non-members (€0.74M) spend almost identically — raising a critical question: Is the loyalty program actually driving incremental revenue? This warrants an immediate redesign review.

---

✅ Recommendations

| Priority | Insight | Recommended Action |
|---|---|---|
| 🔴 High | Loyalty program shows no spending lift | Redesign rewards to incentivize larger cart sizes |
| 🟡 Medium | No dominant hero product | Invest in promoting one category as brand signature |
| 🟢 Ongoing | 25–34 age group leads revenue | Tailor campaigns and offers to this segment |
